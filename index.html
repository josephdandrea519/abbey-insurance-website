Our Story

A family business built on trust,
tide, and community.

Abbey Insurance LLC was founded over 25 years ago by a family who believed insurance should feel personal — not transactional. What started as a shared dream became a thriving independent agency run by siblings Carrie, Erin, and Chris, who work side by side every single day.

When you call Abbey, you're not reaching a call center. You're reaching Carrie, Erin, or Chris — people whose family name is on the door and whose reputation depends entirely on taking care of yours. They know their clients by name, and their clients know them.

Over the past quarter century, they've celebrated births, covered new homes, insured growing businesses, and been present through the hardest moments their clients have ever faced — including the devastating aftermath of Hurricane Sandy. That's not a job. That's a calling.

When Sandy hit,
we didn't leave.

When Hurricane Sandy tore through New Jersey in October 2012, we were right here — not watching from the sidelines, but standing side by side with our clients and our community.

While the floodwaters receded, our phones rang without pause. We worked around the clock helping families file claims, fight for every dollar they were owed, and find a path forward through one of the worst storms this state has ever seen. We didn't just push paperwork — we showed up at flooded homes, sat with people in distress, and made sure no one had to face it alone.

That experience is something we carry with us every single day. It's exactly why we do what we do. When it matters most, we show up. A 1-800 number won't drive to your neighborhood after a disaster. We will.
