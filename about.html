Our Story

A family business built on trust,
tide, and 25 years of community.

Abbey Insurance LLC was founded in 1999 by a family who believed their community deserved an insurance agency that felt like one of their own. Based in Barnegat, NJ — the heart of Ocean County — Abbey has grown from a modest local agency into one of New Jersey's most trusted independent firms, serving clients from Cape May County to Bergen County and everywhere in between.

The agency is run by siblings Carrie, Erin, and Chris. As an independent agency, Abbey is not tied to any single insurance company — which means we work for you, not the carrier. We shop 20+ top-rated companies on every quote to find the right coverage at the right price.

Over 25 years, our team has protected thousands of NJ families, businesses, and renters. They've been there at closings, at accidents, at renewals — and they were there in October 2012 when Hurricane Sandy devastated our coastal community and 200+ of our clients needed us most. That's not a policy number. That's a relationship.

Abbey Insurance is women-owned, BBB A+ accredited, and proud to hold a 4.9-star rating from 312+ verified Google reviews. We carry a NJ Producer License () and operate from our Barnegat office — always available Mon–Fri, 8am–4pm.
