Why NJ Businesses Need the Right Insurance

Running a business in New Jersey comes with real financial risk. A single liability claim, workplace injury, or property loss can threaten everything you've built. New Jersey law mandates workers compensation coverage for virtually all employers — and many clients, landlords, and contracts require proof of general liability before you can even start work.

Abbey Insurance has been protecting NJ small businesses for over 25 years — from one-person startups in Mercer County to multi-location operations in Bergen County and Hudson County. As an independent agency, we shop 20+ carriers to find coverage that fits your industry, your size, and your budget.

Commercial Coverage Types We Shop For You

🏗️

General Liability

Covers third-party bodily injury, property damage, and advertising injury claims. Required by most contracts, landlords, and licensing boards.

📦

Business Owners Policy (BOP)

Bundles general liability and commercial property into one cost-effective package. Ideal for small businesses with a physical location.

👷

Workers Compensation

Required by NJ law for almost all employers. Covers employee medical bills and lost wages for work-related injuries and illnesses.

🚐

Commercial Auto

NJ commercial vehicles need separate coverage from personal auto. Covers trucks, vans, fleets — owned, hired, and non-owned.

💼

Professional Liability

Also called E&O insurance — covers claims of negligence or errors in your professional services. Essential for consultants, advisors, and service businesses.

🔒

Cyber Liability

Covers costs of data breaches, ransomware, and cyber attacks — increasingly essential for any NJ business that handles customer data.

NJ Workers Compensation — What You Need to Know

New Jersey requires workers compensation insurance for virtually every employer — even those with just one part-time employee. Penalties for non-compliance include fines, stop-work orders, and personal liability for work injuries. Coverage must meet NJ statutory benefit requirements. Our agents understand NJ workers comp classification codes and can find competitive rates across our carrier network.
